About Me

Hello! I'm Sanam Acharya, a passionate web developer from Arjundhara-9, Jhapa, Nepal. With a strong enthusiasm for technology and coding, I specialize in creating dynamic and responsive web applications that offer excellent user experiences.

My journey in web development started with a curiosity about how websites work, which quickly grew into a dedicated pursuit of mastering the latest technologies and best practices in the field. I am proficient in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, and I am skilled in version control with Git and GitHub.

Besides coding, I enjoy staying updated with the latest trends in web development, participating in coding challenges, and collaborating with other developers to build innovative projects. My goal is to continually enhance my skills and contribute to creating seamless, user-friendly web applications.

Feel free to connect with me on social media or explore my projects to see some of my work in action. Let's build something amazing together!
